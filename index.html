Character Identity

Class
Pick the class you want to build around. This changes available class skills.
Race
Race helps define sustain, crit, or tankiness. It does not lock your playstyle.
Alliance
Mainly useful for identity, PvP flavor, and character planning.
Primary Role
Choose how this character contributes most: damage, healing, tanking, or hybrid utility.
Content Focus
This tells the app whether to lean toward PvE, PvP, trials, or smaller-group content.
Mode
Use this to signal whether the build is mainly solo, group, or competitive.
Armor Weight
Light usually favors magicka, medium favors stamina, heavy favors survivability.
Build Notes
Write a quick reminder like “trial healer,” “solo questing,” or “PvP bruiser.”
New Player Guide

How to build a character here

Start with Class, Role, and Content Focus. Then go to Attributes & CP, fill your Skills & Bars, and finish with Gear & Sets. The Best-in-Slot and Guild Synergy tabs will react to your choices automatically.

      How gear works here

      Enter your current set names exactly if you know them. The app uses those names to judge whether your setup is selfish, support-oriented, PvP-focused, or group-friendly.
